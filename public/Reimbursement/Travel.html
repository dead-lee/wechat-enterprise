Reimburse the Travel Cos

L&K suggests you to submit reimbursement request in WWER system within 10 working days after returned date.

1. Please read WWER Guideline.

WWER link:https://w3-01.sso.ibm.com/tools/expenses/auth/ibmersbpa.wss

2. Please raise request in WWER system and add "Yi Da YD Zhang/China/IBM" as additional reviewer.

3. Please prepare Hardcopy Documents after you have submitted your WWER request and send to Mail room.
