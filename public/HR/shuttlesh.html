    班车搭乘小提示

    上班时，如发生满员情况，经过所在班车车长确认，可4人共同搭乘出租车前往公司，相关费用可通过WWER进行报销(设置reviewer为 Wang,Fang)；

    Please choose the properly transportation vehicle to company in according to your specific job requirement besides company van.

    • Transportation 1：Take bus 140,187,758,937,966 to Zhengli Rd./Songhu Rd.
    • Transportation 1：Take bus 140,187,537,758,966 to Zhengli Rd./Guoding Rd.
    • Transportation 1：Take line 10 to Jiangwan Stadium/Sanmen Rd.

    Shanghai Subway： http://www.exploreshanghai.com

    Shuttle bus Note:

    Shuttle bus overload on duty, every four employees can take one taxi together to company after bus leader or driver's confirmation. The taxi expenses can be reimbursed through WWER system(set Wang,Fang as reviewer).
